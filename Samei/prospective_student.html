Research Opportunities for Graduate and Undergraduate Students

The Samei group is a leading research-clinical consortium to advance the science and practice of precision medicine in imaging. We are always looking for committed undergraduate and graduate students to become a part of our research group. We have had students from diverse majors, such as Medical Physics, Engineering, Physical Sciences, and Computer Science. Feel free to contact any of us to know more about our lab.
